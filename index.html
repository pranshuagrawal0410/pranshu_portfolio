About Me

Professional background

I am a Chartered Accountancy Finalist and finance professional with experience across audit, financial reporting, taxation, compliance, management reporting, and business operations.

Over the past few years, I have had the opportunity to work with startups, SMEs, and established organizations, helping them strengthen financial processes, maintain compliance, and make better business decisions through accurate reporting and analysis.

My professional journey began with taxation and accounting assignments, where I developed a strong foundation in financial records, regulatory compliance, and client advisory work. As my career progressed, I gained exposure to financial reporting, statutory audits, GST and income tax audits, CMA report preparation, budgeting support, forecasting activities, and business process improvement initiatives.

Alongside traditional finance responsibilities, I developed a strong interest in data analytics and business intelligence. I enjoy working with tools such as Power BI, SQL, Power Query, Advanced Excel, Zoho Books, QuickBooks, Xero, and Tally to transform complex financial and operational data into meaningful insights.

Whether it is building dashboards, identifying trends, analyzing performance, or improving reporting efficiency, I am motivated by solving business problems through a combination of finance, technology, and analytical thinking.

Today, my focus lies at the intersection of Finance and Analytics. I am continuously expanding my knowledge in financial analysis, business intelligence, forecasting, data visualization, and decision-support systems while completing my Chartered Accountancy journey.

Through this portfolio, I showcase projects, dashboards, analyses, and insights that reflect both my professional experience and my commitment to continuous learning.
