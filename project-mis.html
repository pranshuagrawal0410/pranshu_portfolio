Featured Project

Startup Finance Reporting & Management Information System (MIS)

An Excel-based management information system providing revenue tracking, working capital monitoring, budget control, cash runway analysis, and startup risk monitoring for rapidly growing companies.

Category Reporting
Status Completed
Tools Excel
Industry B2B SaaS

Overview

This project addresses critical challenges faced by rapidly growing startups: declining cash visibility, increasing receivables, budget overruns, rising operating costs, and delayed management reporting. Decision-makers require timely and accurate insights to identify risks and take corrective action.

The solution provides a centralized reporting system that consolidates sales, expenses, budgets, and cash flow records into actionable management dashboards with early-warning indicators.

Business Problem

Rapidly growing startups often face challenges such as:

  • Declining cash visibility and uncertain cash runway
  • Increasing receivables and elevated DSO (Days Sales Outstanding)
  • Budget overruns across departments
  • Rising operating costs without proper tracking
  • Delayed management reporting hindering decision-making

Decision-makers require timely and accurate insights to identify risks and take corrective action. This project was developed to address these challenges by creating a centralized reporting solution capable of highlighting key financial trends and operational red flags.

Methodology / Approach

Workflow: Raw Data → Calculations → Management Reporting

  • Consolidated data from sales, receivables, payables, employee costs, budgets, expenses, and cash flow records
  • Built KPI calculation models using Excel formulas and Pivot Tables
  • Created helper tables to transform transaction-level data into management-ready summaries
  • Developed automated dashboards to monitor revenue performance, working capital, budget adherence, and cash health
  • Implemented risk indicators and exception reporting to highlight potential financial concerns

Key Findings

Revenue Concentration Risk

Revenue growth was concentrated in a limited number of products and regions, creating vulnerability to market shifts.

Customer Concentration

A significant portion of revenue originated from a small group of customers, increasing dependency risk.

Receivables Aging Issues

Multiple receivables exceeded standard collection periods resulting in elevated DSO, pressuring working capital.

Budget Overruns

Certain departments consistently exceeded budget allocations, indicating loose budget controls.

Cash Runway Dependency

Cash runway was heavily dependent on maintaining current revenue growth and collection efficiency.

Working Capital Pressure

Working capital performance required continuous monitoring to prevent liquidity constraints.

Recommendations

Working Capital Optimization

  • Strengthen collection procedures
  • Periodic aging reviews
  • Reduce DSO through aggressive follow-up

Budget Management

  • Monthly variance reviews
  • Spending approval controls
  • Department-level variance tracking

Revenue Growth Strategy

  • Diversify customer base
  • Expand high-margin products and regions
  • Monitor customer profitability

Cash Flow Management

  • Maintain minimum runway targets
  • Review burn rate trends monthly
  • Link hiring decisions to cash position

Reporting & Governance

  • Automate MIS generation
  • Standardize KPI definitions
  • Dashboard risk alerts
