Overview

This project involves building a compliance-focused dashboard for GST reporting, tracking, and exception analysis. The dashboard will provide comprehensive views for liability tracking, filing trend review, and mismatch detection.

The solution aims to track GST position and identify filing issues early, enabling proactive compliance management and reducing regulatory risk.

Business Problem

Organizations facing GST compliance challenges need:

  • Real-time visibility of GST liability positions
  • Tracking of filing deadlines and compliance status
  • Early detection of reconciliation mismatches
  • Analysis of filing trends and patterns
  • Exception reporting for audit preparation
  • Centralized GST data from returns and registers

Without proper tracking systems, organizations risk filing delays, mismatch penalties, and compliance gaps. This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing automated monitoring and exception alerts.
