Overview

This project involves designing a future-state financial model for reporting and financing needs. The CMA (Cost and Management Accountant) projection model will provide comprehensive financial forecasting capabilities to support credit decisions and financial planning.

The model will include projected income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements based on historical financials and strategic assumptions.

Business Problem

Organizations requiring credit funding or financial planning need:

  • Accurate future-state financial projections for lender review
  • Scenario planning capabilities to evaluate different business outcomes
  • Comprehensive CMA reports for regulatory and financing purposes
  • Structured forecasting models based on historical data and assumptions
  • Ability to test conservative vs. aggressive business scenarios

This project was developed to address these needs by creating a structured projection model that supports credit and financial planning decisions.

Methodology / Approach

Designed a future-state model for reporting and financing needs using structured financial modeling techniques.

  • Analyzed historical financial data to establish baseline trends
  • Developed revenue projection models based on growth assumptions
  • Built cost projection models with COGS and operating expense forecasts
  • Created working capital models for balance sheet projections
  • Integrated cash flow statements linking all three projection sheets
  • Implemented scenario testing for conservative vs. aggressive cases
  • Added sensitivity analysis for key assumptions

Key Features (Planned)

  • Three-statement financial model (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
  • Multi-year projection capability (3-5 years typical)
  • Scenario comparison tables (Conservative vs. Base vs. Aggressive)
  • Sensitivity analysis for key variables
  • Automated financial ratio calculations
  • Debt service coverage and liquidity metrics
  • Professional formatting for lender presentation
  • Documentation of all assumptions

Expected Key Findings

Planned model will support scenario planning and lender review with insights including:

  • Projected revenue growth trajectories under different scenarios
  • Future profitability margins and EBITDA trends
  • Working capital requirements and cash conversion needs
  • Debt service capacity and coverage ratios
  • Funding requirements and capital structure implications

Recommendations (Planned)

  • Use conservative assumptions for lender-facing projections
  • Perform thorough scenario testing before finalizing projections
  • Document all assumptions clearly for audit trail
  • Validate projections against industry benchmarks
  • Update models regularly as actuals become available
